THE EARTH

Earth, third planet from the Sun and the fifth largest in the solar system in terms of size and mass. Its single most outstanding feature is that its near-surface environments are the only places in the universe known to harbour life. It is designated by the symbol ⊕. Earth’s name in English, the international language of astronomy, derives from Old English and Germanic words for ground and earth. It is the only name for a planet in the solar system that does not come from Greco-Roman mythology.
